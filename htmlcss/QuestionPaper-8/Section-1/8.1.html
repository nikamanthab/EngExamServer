IELTS Listening Sample 8. Section 1

This is section 1 of IELTS Listening practice test #8. The Listening test consists of 4 sections and lasts 40 minutes. After you finish the first section, click 'check' and move on to the next section.

A Japanese girl and a housing officer are talking about her homestay family.
