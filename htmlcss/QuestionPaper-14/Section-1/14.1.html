IELTS Listening Sample 14. Section 1

Here you can find IELTS Listening test #14. Use it for IELTS Listening practice: listen to the audio and answer the questions online. After you complete this section, press "check" and you'll see which questions you answered correctly and which you got wrong. Then, you can proceed to the next section. After you complete all four sections, your score for IELTS Listening test will be automatically calculated.

This module is the same for Academic and General IELTS.

The Listening module takes 40 minutes: 30 min for testing and 10 min for transferring your answers to the answer sheet.
