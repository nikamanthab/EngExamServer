IELTS Listening Sample 13. Section 2

Listen to the audio and answer the questions. When you finish, click 'check' and proceed to the next section.

The Listening module takes 40 minutes: 30 min for testing and 10 min for transferring your answers to.


 

SECTION 2. QUESTIONS 11-20


Questions 11-14

Answer the questions below. Choose the correct letter, A, B, or C.

Online Exchange Business

11. The website is organised by

  1. family memebrs.
  2. friends.
  3. businessmen.


12. How long has the website been operated?

  1. about 3 weeks.
  2. about 3 months.
  3. about 6 months.


13. How many registered users are there in the website?

  1. 1000.
  2. 1500.
  3. 2000.


14. Which country has the most users in the website?

  1. Ireland.
  2. UK.
  3. Canada.


Questions 15-16

Choose TWO letters, A-E

Which TWO things are most popular among users?

A Children's books.

В Textbooks.

С Computer games.

D Toys.

E Tools.

Write ONE WORD ONLY for each answer.
